Private by design

A free, open-source QR code generator that works offline

SayaQR creates QR codes entirely in your browser. Your links, passwords, contact details, payment information, and files are never sent to a server.

Is SayaQR private?

Yes. QR content is processed locally on your device with no tracking, accounts, analytics, content uploads, or remote generation API.

Can I use it offline?

Yes. SayaQR is an installable progressive web app. After the first visit, its app shell is cached for offline QR creation and export.

What QR codes and formats are supported?

Create QR codes for text, URLs, Wi-Fi, email, SMS, phone, vCard contacts, UPI payments, calendar events, and locations. Export SVG, PNG, WebP, PDF, or batch ZIP files.

Is SayaQR open source?

Yes. SayaQR is MIT-licenseable and its source is available on GitHub. QR encoding uses Nayuki's MIT-licensed QR Code generator library.
